Internet

E-mail

Email is an electronic communication method where users have unique addresses to send and receive messages. It enables the exchange of text, files, and media over the internet. Messages are transmitted to recipients' email servers and can be accessed using clients or web-based services. It is widely used for personal and professional communication worldwide.

Wi-Fi

Wi-Fi, short for "Wireless Fidelity," is a wireless communication technology that enables devices to connect to the internet or communicate with each other without the need for wired connections. It utilizes radio waves and allows for convenient and flexible connectivity in homes, offices, and public spaces.

Router

A router is a networking device that connects multiple devices in a network and facilitates data transfer between them. It provides internet connectivity, acts as a gateway between the local network and the internet, and uses routing protocols to direct data packets to their intended destinations.
